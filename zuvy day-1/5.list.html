Angela's Recipe for the Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons groung cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, suger, eggs.
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. add in the flour and mix until comblined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough.
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
  6. Mix the milk with the yeast, suger, eggs.
